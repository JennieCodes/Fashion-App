How to dress for your body type!

Hello boys! Your bodies come in different shapes and sizes like everyone else. Knowing your body shape and features will take your fashion sense to the next level! Please keep in mind, that unlike women, men do not have as many clothing styled options and most of the clothing I will be mentioning will interlap with one another. Once you know your body type it will become much easier to figure out which types and styles of clothing would impact your appearance positively or negatively. The better your style is the better your confidence will be too! Now, let's get into it! Please select your body shape down below:


Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

Inverted Triangle Similar to the trapezoid/hourglass body type, you have a broad shoulder and chest, but narrow waist and hip. Your upper body is wider than your lower body, mainly seen with atheletes. Fun fact, it is the rarest body shape. You don't need to balance your top and bottom proportions for this body type.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Straight fit
  • 2. Relaxed fit
  • 3. Clothing that will balance your broad shoulders
  • 4. V neck tees narrow your broad shoulders
  • 5. Graphic tees will give focus to other areas
    other than your shoulders
  • 6. Avoid scoop necklines
  • 7. Avoid skinny fit
Celebrities with a inverted triangle body:
  • Usain Bolt
  • Jason Statham
  • The Rock
  • Vin Diesel
  • Taylor Lautner

Rectangle If you have a rectangle body type, your shoulders are the same width as your waist and hips. You should aim to widen your shoulder making your torso area appear more narrow.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Structure blazers and jackets with padded shoulders
  • 2. Layering shirts
  • 3. Knitted wear worn over shirts to widen shoulder/chest area
  • 4. Emphasize on prints and patterns
  • 5. Avoid geometric prints and photographic tees
  • 6. Avoid color block styled shirts
  • 7. Avoid sleevless shirts
Celebrities with a rectangle body:
  • David Beckham
  • Harry Styles
  • Hugh Grant
  • Ashton Kutcher

Round The round or oval body type is pretty self-explanatory, and is actually one of the more common body types. The round or oval shape typically appears in the middle portion of your body. You typically have narrower shoulder and slimmer legs, so try to focus more on your shoulder to be more defined.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Vertical and thin stripes making you appear longer
  • 2. Pants inseam and sleeve lengths are at the correct lengths
  • 3. Loosely fitted pants to lengthen your legs
  • 4. Avoid horizontal stripes
  • 5. Avoid crew necks and polos shirts
  • 6. Avoid tight or slim fit clothing
Celebrities with a round body:
  • James Corden
  • Elton John
  • Boris Johnson
  • Zach Galifianakis

Hourglass The hourglass body type in men are typically known as trapezoid or rhomboid. It actually is the second rarest of body shapes with just 13% of men that fall into this category. Men with a trapezoid or rhomboid body type has broad shoulders and chest, but they have smaller waists and hips, similarly to the inverted triangle. If you have this body type, your body type fits MOST clothing woohoo! Most clothing will look proportional on you and you will be able to stay with all the new trends.

What can you wear? Literally anything, there are no restrictions due to the upper and lower body being well-proportioned.
Celebrities with a hourglass body:
  • Hugh Jackman
  • Gerard Butler
  • Daniel Craig

Triangle The triangle body type is the exact opposite of an inverted triangle. This body type is the definition of a "dad bod". But who doesn't love a dad bod?? Your shoulders and chest are narrower than your waist, which in turn, makes your bottom half appear larger. People with this body shape typically has a sloping shoulder line, which creates an inbalance of the lower body being wider than the upper body.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Focus on well-fitted clothes
  • 2. Straight-leg pants and Chino-like trousers
  • 3. Structured blazers and jackets that give shape to your shoulders
  • 4. Darker colors
  • 5. Avoid horizontal stripes around the stomach area
  • 6. Avoid wearing jackets that pockets too close to the stomach

Celebrities with a hourglass body:
  • Jack Black
  • Alec Baldwin
  • Adam Devine

How to dress for your body type!

Everyone comes in different shapes and sizes! Here's a secret, no one looks good in everything. Body shape is not necessarily something you can change with dieting and working out; genetic plays a huge role in the way we look as well. Before you continue to read, let's clear this something up, no one body shape is better than another body shape. Love yourself and be who you are! I am here today to show you that what you wear can greatly impact the way your body looks. You can wear something one day that compliments your body so much,and the next day it looks like you've lost or gained 50lbs! So let's find out what you can wear to compliment that beautiful body shall we? Please select your body shape down below:

Inverted Triangle If you have an inverted triangle body shape, your shoulders are typically wider than your hips. You probably do not have definition between your waist and your hip.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Opposite of triangle body shape
  • 2. Minimize the effect of the upper body
  • 3. Darker colors on the upper body
  • 4. Lighter colors on the lower body
  • 5. Draw attention AWAY from the upper half
  • 6. Avoid necklines like "boat-neck" or Bardot, big straps,
    halter tops, big shawl collars, and larger patterns
    on your upper body.
  • 7. Avoid accessories
  • 8. Wider skirts(a-line skirt) or wide trousers
  • 9. The best pair of jeans to wear would be boyfriend jeans
Celebrities with a inverted triangle body:
  • Angelina Jolie
  • Demi Moore
  • Rene Zellweger
  • Naomi Campbell
  • Emily VanCamp

Rectangle If you have a rectangle body shape, you typically wear the same size tops and bottoms. You don't have an overly defined waistline, and have a straight shoulder line and hips. The rectangle body shape is usually called the "athetlic" body shape.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Create an illusion of a waist
  • 2. Belts around your waist
  • 3. Mid-waisted trousers/pants
  • 4. Belted jackets
  • 5. Avoid stiffer material, use softer fabrics
  • 6. Avoid silhouettes that are too vertical or straight
  • 7. You can typicall pull off any pair of pants; loose, form-fitting,
    even body-conscious pants (show those curves girl!)
Celebrities with a inverted triangle body:
  • Cameron Diaz
  • Hilary Duff
  • Kate Hudson
  • Gwyneth Paltrow
  • Natalie Portman

Round The round body shape, also known as the apple body shape, is known for the larger upper body than lower body. Round body shapes typically have no defined waistline. Because this body shape is usually the most difficult to buy for, we tend to oversize too much to compensate the broader upper body. Proportional dressing is recommended!

What can you wear?
  • 1. Avoid oversized and bodycon fitted outfits
  • 2. Keep your tops and jackets more simple
  • 3. Avoid shoulder details (shoulder pads, etc)
  • 4. Aim for a more balanced looks(well-fitted,
    tailored tops, and jackets that aren't too tight)
  • 5. Avoid body-conscious dresses (slim-fit dress that
    goes above knee)
  • 6. Straight leg jeans
  • 7. Pencil skirts with a loose fitted top or loose sweater
Celebrities with a round body:
  • Gabourey Sidibe
  • Sonakshi Sinha
  • Queen Latifah
  • Oprah Winfrey
  • Kate Winslet

Hourglass An hourglass body shape literally looks like an hourglass! They have equal sized hips and shoulder with a well-defined waist. If you have this body shape, without a doubt you'd want to accentuate your waist as it is the slimmest part of your body. Hourglass shapes tends to come with a more busty figure. You can flaunt it or you can minimize it with a boatneck, v-neck.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Clothing that draws attention to the waist
  • 2. Avoid shapeless or baggy clothing
  • 3. V-necks, scoop necks, boatnecks for less busty look
  • 4. Avoid turtlenecks
  • 5. Angled pocket pants, instead of inseams pockets
  • 6. Miniskirts are a great option to show off your legs
  • 7. Peplum tops and empire waist dresses
Celebrities with a hourglass body:
  • Marilyn Monroe
  • Beyonce
  • Scarlett Johansson
  • Sofia Vergara
  • Elizabeth Taylor

Triangle The triangle body shape, also known as the pear, is identified by a wider lower half, with a narrower top half. For this body shape, you want to draw the attention to your upper half and your waist. You have a defined waist due to your larger hips. Becuase your bottom half is heavier than your upper body, it creates an imbalance. Emphasis on the waist and de-emphasize on the lower half to create a more balanced silhouette.

What can you wear?
  • 1. Darker colors on your lower body
  • 2. Lighter colors for the uppder body
  • 3. Oversized tops and jacket
  • 4. Skinny jeans would be the best jeans for this body shape
  • 5. Avoid clothing that emphasizes on the hips
  • 6. Avoid one piece dresses, separate dresses are recommended
